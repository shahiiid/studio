Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need an Interior Designer or Decorator?

Whether you need a little help or a lot, from answers to a few questions on color selection or furniture layout to help on planning and completing a complicated remodel or new construction project, the advice and guidance of an Interior Designer is invaluable.

Having a professional Interior Designer on your team who has the knowledge and experience of countless similar projects as well as the information and expertise for finding the exact products and trades-people you need to get the best result for your project is priceless. Working with an Interior Designer will help you to move seamlessly and efficiently through the project, as well as help you prevent costly mistakes or side-steps along the way.

When do I need an Interior Designer?

While a good designer can help you at any stage of the design, it is most advantageous for you to begin your work with a designer at the earliest stages of the design. As with almost any process, a good plan in place as early as possible is the best route to success. Makeover Design Studio can take your project from the initial point of gathering information on the requirements and needs that you have for your space, throughout conception, development, construction, purchasing and installation for every detail of your project – all the way to completion, the final result that shows the great result of a plan well thought out and well executed from the initial ideas to the final details.

What is your Fee Schedule?

Our fees are based on the scope of work and we, at Makeover Design Studio, charge a simple flat design fee when possible.

How long will it take to design and build my project?

Completion of the project depends upon various factors like the scope of work, complexity of the design, your own ability to make decisions using choices provided by us, time at your disposal and flow of money. The earlier you can contact us, the better off we'll be. We will let you know the expected date in prior to start the work.
